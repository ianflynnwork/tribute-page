Brian Eno

Musician, Composer, Record Producer, Visual Artist, Pioneer

Eno has been actively creating and producing music since the 1970's. He has produced over 50 albums for other artists, wrote or co-wrote around 40 albums personally, and been credited and worked on countless others. He is the Godfather of Ambient Music, co-founded the band Roxy Music, and has worked with a diverse group of artists including Talking Heads, David Bowie, Cold Play, Grace Jones, U2, Devo and Depeche Mode to name a few.
